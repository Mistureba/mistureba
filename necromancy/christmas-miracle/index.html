Or should I say fiends?

Every year, what gets me out of bed is the certainty that Ocarina of Time will be recognized as the masterpiece it is in the GameFAQs Best Game Ever poll. So much changes in the world of games, yet every year we come and say thanks for the gift that Shigeru Miyamoto bestowed upon us. In a world of F2P scams and the death of innovation in games, it is a reassurance of the highest caliber for gamers to speak their voices and say what matters most: quality.

And now, this. I thought Undertale was an OK game, but the cult that has sprung up around it is straight out of 1984, down to the Orwellian doublespeak. Do you truly think Undertale is the best game ever? No, you've drank the Kool-Aid. You've mocked the works of the masters.

Thanks a lot. Christmas is over.
